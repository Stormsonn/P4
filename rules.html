Rules

  • Be Respectful to Everyone
  • 115 points for 10-11 mons for Upper Div (Div A)
  • You can have 1-3 Tera Captains totaling 20 points; no tera captains can be worth more than 10 pts; must declare Tera Captains by end of Draft.
  • All smogon clauses are in effect (Sleep Clause, Evasion Clause, etc). Standard rules apply (all mons must be level 100, etc.)
  • All complex bans are listed in the tiers. No Moody, Shadow Tag, Evasion abilities, etc.
  • All battles must be done under the Nat Dex Draft format on Showdown, found under "Draft" Category. Someone must post link in #live-battles after completion.
  • Timer should only be on if both coaches agree or if a mod says so
  • Z-Moves, Dynamax, and Gigantamax are banned. Maximum 1 Mega Pokemon.
  • All cut moves besides HIdden Power and Pursuit are allowed
  • Tiers are not final until the day of the offical draft. All NFE's and bad mons not listed are probably 1 pt, DM Kwadzki if you're unsure (I hid that column bc it was long)
  • Regieleki AND Shedinja cannot be Tera Captains
  • All battles must be completed within 4 weeks of the official start of Season 3. It is up to you to schedule your matches, and we will not be using a weekly schedule, but rather leaving it up to you to play each of your matches. You will play each team round robin within your division.
  • Kwad's Stupid Rule 2.0: Dirty Delibird; prior to draft we play a game of "dirty santa:"/"white elephant" with high value Pokemon. Afterwards, you can choose to keep that mon or return it to FA. If you keep it, it will count towards your budget.
  • Rifk's Stupid Rule: Mono Min & Max: Must draft a minimum of 25pts worth of the same type on your team by the end of draft, maximum of 35pts worth of the same type.
